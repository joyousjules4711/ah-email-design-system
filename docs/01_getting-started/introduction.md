# Design Philosophy

> **Designed for Every Student Journey.**

---

## Purpose

The Akademikerhilfe Email Design System is more than a collection of HTML templates.

It provides a shared framework for creating consistent, accessible and maintainable transactional emails across the entire organisation.

Every design decision should contribute to a better experience for students while reducing complexity for internal teams.

---

# Our Mission

Every email represents Akademikerhilfe.

Whether a student receives a housing offer, a payment reminder or a move-in confirmation, every communication should feel familiar, trustworthy and professional.

The Email Design System ensures a consistent user experience across all departments and throughout the entire student journey.

---

# Design Principles

The Email Design System is built around six core principles.

---

## 1. Human First

Emails are written for people.

Use clear and friendly language.

Avoid unnecessary technical or bureaucratic wording whenever possible.

Legal terminology should only be used where required.

---

## 2. Consistency

Every email should feel like Akademikerhilfe.

The same visual language, typography, spacing and tone of voice should be used throughout the entire system.

Users should immediately recognize an Akademikerhilfe email.

---

## 3. Simplicity

Good communication removes complexity.

Every email should focus on one primary message.

Avoid unnecessary information and visual distractions.

---

## 4. Accessibility

Emails should be usable by everyone.

Layouts, colors, typography and HTML should follow accessibility best practices whenever technically possible.

Accessibility is considered from the beginning—not added later.

---

## 5. Reusability

The system should consist of reusable components instead of individual email templates.

Components should be combined to build templates.

Templates should not duplicate components.

---

## 6. Maintainability

The design system should reduce long-term maintenance.

Changes should be implemented once and reused across all templates whenever possible.

The system should become easier to maintain over time—not more complex.

---

# Communication Principles

Every email should be:

- Clear
- Helpful
- Professional
- Friendly
- Trustworthy
- Action-oriented

---

# Writing Principles

Write in a modern, service-oriented tone.

Prefer active voice over passive voice.

Keep sentences concise and easy to understand.

Use legal terminology only where legally required.

Explain processes before presenting exceptions.

Always provide the recipient with a clear next step.

---

# Design Principles

Visual design should support communication.

Design should never compete with content.

Whitespace improves readability.

Hierarchy should guide attention.

Color should emphasize—not decorate.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Email Design System is intended to become the single source of truth for all transactional email communication at Akademikerhilfe.

Over time, additional communication channels may adopt the same design principles and reusable components.

---

# Guiding Statement

> **We build communication, not emails.**

Every component, template and design decision should improve the experience for both students and employees.
