# 🏠 Akademikerhilfe Email Design System

> **Designed for Every Student Journey.**

The **Akademikerhilfe Email Design System** is the central source of truth for all transactional email communication across Akademikerhilfe.

It provides a unified framework for designing, developing and maintaining modern, responsive and accessible email templates while ensuring consistency with the Akademikerhilfe Corporate Design.

The design system serves as a shared foundation for developers, designers and business departments, enabling consistent communication throughout every stage of the student journey.

---

# Vision

Every email sent by Akademikerhilfe should feel familiar.

Whether a student receives

- a housing offer,
- a room assignment,
- a payment reminder,
- a SEPA pre-notification,
- a move-in confirmation,
- a contract amendment,
- or a deposit settlement,

every communication should feel like it belongs to the same ecosystem.

The goal of this project is not simply to redesign existing emails.

The goal is to build a scalable Email Design System that supports every communication touchpoint between Akademikerhilfe and its residents.

---

# Objectives

The Email Design System is built around six primary objectives.

## 🎨 Consistency

Provide a unified visual identity across all transactional emails.

---

## 🧩 Reusability

Build reusable HTML components that can be combined into complete email templates.

---

## 📱 Responsiveness

Ensure every email provides an excellent reading experience on desktop and mobile devices.

---

## ♿ Accessibility

Follow email accessibility best practices whenever technically possible.

---

## 🌍 Multilingual Support

Support German and English templates while maintaining a single design language.

---

## 🔧 Maintainability

Reduce long-term maintenance by standardizing layouts, components and styling.

---

# Scope

The project currently covers

- 150+ master email templates
- German and English versions
- department-specific templates
- residence-specific variants
- transactional communication only

Marketing newsletters are currently outside the scope of this project.

---

# Design Principles

The design system is guided by five principles.

## Clear

Every email should communicate one primary message.

Information should be easy to scan.

---

## Human

Write for people.

Avoid technical language whenever possible.

---

## Helpful

Every email should answer the user's next question before it is asked.

---

## Consistent

Visual appearance and language should be consistent across all departments.

---

## Accessible

Emails should work across common email clients and devices.
