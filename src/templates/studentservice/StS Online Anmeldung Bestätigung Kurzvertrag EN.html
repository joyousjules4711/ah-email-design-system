What happens next?

We will let you know as soon as possible whether a room is available. As soon as we can make you an offer or provide an update regarding room availability, we will contact you by email.

Please understand that we cannot give an exact date for a possible offer, as this depends on the number of applications and cancellations.

Please stay connected

We will regularly send you an email containing a confirmation link so that we can verify whether your application is still current.

If you do not confirm your continued interest within the stated deadline, we will assume that you no longer require accommodation and will remove your application from the waiting list.

Please therefore check your email regularly, including your spam or junk folder.
