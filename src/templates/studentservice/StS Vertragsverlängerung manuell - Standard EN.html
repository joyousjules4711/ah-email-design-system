Changes to the residence regulations

Please note the following additions to the residence regulations, which form an integral part of your housing contract.

V. Principles governing the use of residences

Residence places are rooms made available to residents as living accommodation. Depending on the room category, they may be used individually or jointly and include balconies, terraces and loggias.

(2) Smoking ban

Smoking is prohibited in all Akademikerhilfe student residences in order to protect non-smokers.

This prohibition applies to tobacco products and related products, including electronic cigarettes, herbal smoking products, heated tobacco products and water pipes.

(21) Notices in the residences

Notices issued by Akademikerhilfe are intended to inform residents. Other notices and advertising materials must originate from the residents' representation in coordination with residence management or must have explicit approval from Akademikerhilfe.

Your room preferences

All submitted room preferences will be processed during room allocation by July at the latest. If your preferred room can be allocated, we will inform you separately by email. If not, you will automatically remain in your current room.

Outstanding residence fees

If residence fees remain unpaid, Akademikerhilfe may revoke the contract extension. In that case, the accounting team will contact you by sending a payment reminder.
