Information about the refund of your deposit

At the beginning of your contract, you paid a deposit. For the refund, please ensure that your bank details in our Online Portal are up to date.

Please note that any outstanding claims, such as unpaid accommodation fees, may be deducted from your room deposit where applicable. The remaining amount will be refunded without interest.

Akademikerhilfe will arrange the refund of the deposit, less any outstanding claims, no earlier than 45 days and no later than 60 days after the end of the contract.

If you have provided a SEPA direct debit mandate, we will transfer the amount to that account. If you would like the refund to be made to a different account, or if you have not provided a SEPA mandate, please enter the account details in the Online Portal.
