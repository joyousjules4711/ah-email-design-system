Cancelling your reserved residence place

• You may cancel the reserved residence place without a cancellation fee up to 62 days before the contract begins.
• If you withdraw between 61 days and one day before the contract begins, Akademikerhilfe will retain one monthly residence fee as a cancellation charge. This amount will be deducted from your deposit, and any remaining balance will be refunded.

Please send your cancellation request by email to studentservice@akademikerhilfe.at . You are responsible for providing proof that the cancellation request was received in time.
